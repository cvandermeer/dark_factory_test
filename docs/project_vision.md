There is a adventure game based on the adventure quest.
Info about adventure quest

AdventureQuest is a long-running browser-based fantasy RPG released in 2002 by Artix Entertainment
. It mixes turn-based combat, quest-driven storytelling, humor, and collectible gear in a style inspired by classic single-player RPGs and tabletop fantasy adventures.

What the game is about

You play as a customizable hero exploring a fantasy world filled with dragons, undead armies, elemental realms, pirates, demons, and parody characters. The story evolves through episodic quests and seasonal events, with new content added regularly over the years.

Core gameplay
Turn-based battles
Combat happens one turn at a time. You choose attacks, spells, pets, or defensive actions while enemies respond with their own moves.
Character classes
Players can train in classes like:
Warrior
Mage
Rogue
Paladin
Necromancer
Ninja
DragonLord
Each class has unique skills and playstyles.
Equipment and customization
The game has thousands of weapons, armors, shields, pets, and spells. Many items have elemental properties that affect combat strategy.
Leveling and stats
You gain experience and gold from battles, then improve stats such as Strength, Dexterity, Intellect, and Endurance.
Story and events
AdventureQuest is known for humorous writing, holiday events, and large story arcs involving recurring villains and world-ending threats.
Art style and tone

The game uses colorful 2D artwork with animated battles and exaggerated fantasy designs. Its tone shifts between epic fantasy and comedy, often parodying pop culture and RPG clichés.
